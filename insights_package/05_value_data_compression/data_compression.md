# The Value of Data Compression
## Signals, Actions, and the Architecture of Regulatory Defensibility

Data volume is a vanity metric. Real enterprise value is found in the **compression ratio**—how fast you turn chaotic signals into definitive actions.

![FinOps Engine](../images/finops_engine.png)

Compliance and risk officers do not audit features; they audit the decision trail. By architecting immutable audit logs and lineage mapping, we turn AI from a "black box" liability into a defensible, high-margin enterprise engine.
