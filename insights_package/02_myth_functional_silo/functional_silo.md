# The Myth of the Functional Silo
## Why Modern Tech Leadership Requires Total Convergence of Product, Data, and Analytics

Segregating Product, Data, and Analytics into silos creates three failure modes: 
1. Product without Data = Guesswork.
2. Data without Analytics = A COGS Drain.
3. Analytics without Product = A Science Project.

![Silo Breakdown](../images/silo_breakdown.png)

Total convergence is the only way to compress Time-to-Value. Modern leadership isn't about managing isolated teams; it's about converging these three vectors to de-risk capital allocation and maximize enterprise value.
